All lives on the earth deserve to live a happy and peaceful life. As we know some are blessed a bit more in terms of finance than the others. Money that provides pleasure to one could save a life of the other. moneyism strives to connect those good hearts with less privileged people to make an impact on lives.

moneyism provides a platform where people can raise funds by creating fundraisers, getting support from others and to reach the donors.

The organization is run with the help of volunteers and supporters to ensure that the money donated for a fundraiser is given to the fundraiser with as less charges as possible.
